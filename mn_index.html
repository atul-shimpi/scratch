Important notes about precertification and the use of this tool:

This tool does not reflect benefits coverage* nor does it include an exhaustive listing of all Non-covered Services (i.e., experimental procedures, cosmetic surgery, etc.) as outlined within your provider manual.

To determine coverage:
Step 1:
  • Access eligibility and benefits information on the Availity Web Portal or
  • Call Provider Services at [1-800-901-0020 (Medicaid/FAMIS) or 1-855-817-5788 (MMP)]
Step 2:

Use prior authorization tool

Step 3:

If procedure requires preauthorization, visit our Avality portal.

To request authorizations:

  1. From the Availity home page, select Patient Registration from the top navigation.
  2. Select Auth/Referral Inquiry or Authorizations.

*Services may be listed that are not covered benefits, whether they do or do not require precertification. Verify benefit coverage prior to rendering services.
